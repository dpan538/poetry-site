I arrived at the era, to be displayed.
Like a greeting card printed in gray dust,
playing on the shelves, faded yet stayed.

The snacks in hand, the rubber in mouth,
in the place within your heart,
value defined by material and appearance.

Growing up, not yet mature, a chapter of life.
Together with a cat,
shaped by emotions into a sphere,
transformed into daily, monthly, yearly stories.

From newspapers,
Written into memories.
