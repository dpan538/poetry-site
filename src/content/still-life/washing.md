firstLine: "At five, I get up from the boat, washing; Seven, washing, waking up, washing."
---
At five, I get up from the boat, washing;
Seven, washing, waking up, washing.
Getting out of bed, washing.

When water flows over my head,
In the bottle, from the sky,
The clear water gently drips, dripping,
Warm all the way to my toes,
Touching my entire body, waking up, washing.

Going out, washing; back home, washing;
Eating, washing; sleeping, washing.
Today, washing; tomorrow, washing.

Washing.
